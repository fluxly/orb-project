The Open Redaction Blocks (ORB) Project

ORB of the week



Block #27

The mission of the ORB Project is to provide an open set of data and visual assets to allow artists and designers easy access to ORBs (Open Redaction Blocks) for use in their creative works. All ORB assets and data are released under a Creative Commons license, and are available for free to the public at "http://orb-project.us" and on Github.
On this site you can view and download individual blocks from the gallery. We will also highlight creative projects using the blocks, as they appear.
